Our Mission

By Grace Charity provides funding for families living in poverty enabling families to start a small business and create a livelihood.

The central objective, in addition to breaking the cycle of poverty, is to use By Grace Charity private funds to create a community where the families receiving the gift have an understanding of how to help another family.

Once a family has experienced the excitement of earning much needed income, the concept is to PASS IT ON! Over a period of a few months, persons living in poverty are transformed into donors.

Small Business Tailor Shops

Women in the community receive supplies and instruction from By Grace Charity to start their own seamstress shops. This enables them to provide school uniforms and other clothing at a reduced cost to families with limited resources. These industrious women also seek out others in their community to create new businesses. More info...

Small Business Poultry Farming

Qualifying families receive poultry, feed and wire mesh enclosures. These families, live in close proximity and have daily or weekly contact allowing them to form a village Co-Op to market the eggs and purchase supplies in bulk. They agree in advance to donate surplus chicks or layers to another qualifying family in need. The new families agree to do the same. More info...

Small Business Fish Farming

Fish farming allows impoverished families to become self reliant in a very short period of time. Families are given basic supplies such as plastic liners, feed, and fish fry to raise tilapia on their own land. They market the fish to provide much needed resource for their families, and assist new families in starting their own fish farms. More info...
